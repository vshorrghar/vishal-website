About Me

I'm a Cloud Solutions Architect based in Copenhagen, helping Nordic and EMEA enterprises adopt cloud, Kubernetes, and GenAI. I work across Denmark, Sweden, Finland, and the broader Nordics — with enterprise, SMB, startups, and greenfield customers across multiple industries. Previously as an AWS Technical Account Manager, I ran business reviews, drove operational excellence, and helped customers optimize cloud costs across their portfolios.

Before AWS, I was the OpenShift architect for a Nordic company handling payment gateways and government identity platforms on those clusters — with 99.99% uptime and PCI-DSS compliance. Before that, I worked at Portworx/Pure Storage doing QA for their Kubernetes storage solution, testing across multiple clouds and releasing on AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure marketplaces. And at Radisys, I was the Kubernetes infrastructure architect for one of India's biggest broadband platforms — built for Reliance JIO and now serving millions of customers in production.

My roots are in high-speed data centers. I validated 10/40G iSCSI, FCoE, and RDMA adapters at Chelsio Communications, then worked on Cisco's UCS blade & rack platform. I've worked with servers from Dell, HPE, IBM, Supermicro — managed SAN/NAS networking, enterprise storage (NetApp, EMC), and Kubernetes storage (Portworx). I started as a technical trainer for Linux and CCNA, then managed worldwide servers and databases for a wireless ISP. My career evolved naturally: data centers → virtualization → Kubernetes & containers → cloud → GenAI. Today I'm 4x AWS Certified and recognized as AWS Kubernetes & Containers Ambassador for the Nordic region.
